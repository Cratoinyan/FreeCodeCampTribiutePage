Life of Tolkien

  • 1892 Born in Bloemfentontein, Orange Free State
  • 1895 Moved to England when his father died
  • 1903 He won a Foundation Scholarship and returned to King Edward's
  • 1904 Taken to Birmingham Oratory when his mother died
  • 1915 He got elected as second lieutenant in the Lancashire Fusiliers
  • 1917 After he returned from war he started working on The Book of Lost Tales
  • 1920 He started working at Oxford English Dictionary and wrote The Hobbit and first two volumes of The Lord of the Rings
  • 1959 He retired and moved to Bournemouth
  • 1973 He passed away
